Pan-Fried Salmon and Veggies

Healthy and light, filling too, it's just right!

Pan-Fried salmon and veggies is full of wonderful texture.

The taste is totally out of this world, learn how to make it now!

Ingredients:

  • Salmon Fillet
  • Peas
  • Corn Kernels
  • Stick of Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  1. Pre-heat a cast iron pan with a small Stick of Butter thrown in
  2. Prepare a pot of boiled water
  3. Pour Peas and Corn Kernels into the pot of boiled water
  4. Put down the Salmon Fillet and sear all sides without skin, until the meat turns white.
  5. Once the pot of water starts bubbling, your Peas and Corn Kernels are done.
  6. Present both food on your plate
  7. Sprinkle Salt and Pepper
  8. Congratulations, you are done!

Enjoy this healthy and refreshing meal!
