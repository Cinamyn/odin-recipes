Samgyeopsal Sukju Bokkeum

(삼겹살 숙주 보끔)

The world's best gourmet dish!

A rather simple dish made from the finest ingredients in the land.

Following our simple method, you too, will be able to make this delectable treat.

Ingredients:

  • Porkbelly Strips
  • Bean Sprouts
  • Spring Onion
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Pepper
  1. Pre-heat a dry cast iron pan
  2. Place frozen Porkbelly Strips into pan
  3. Once Porkbelly Strips are soft, add Bean Sprouts
  4. Once Bean Sprouts are soft, add Spring Onion
  5. Quickly add Oyster Sauce and Pepper
  6. Let it cook until looks brown and seared
  7. Congratulations, you are done!

Enjoy the greatest dish in the world!
