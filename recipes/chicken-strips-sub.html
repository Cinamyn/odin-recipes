Chicken Strips Sub

The perfect lunch everyday!

A blend of delicious ingredients on a warm, toasty bread roll.

What more could you want?

Ingredients:

  • White Bread Roll
  • Sliced Chicken Strips
  • Sliced Cucumber
  • Sliced Tomato
  • Diced Carrot
  • Lettuce
  • Spinach
  • Chipotle Sauce
  1. Cut open your White Bread Roll.
  2. Place in an oven or toaster and gently toast for 30 seconds.
  3. Place Lettuce and Spinach.
  4. Next, place Sliced Tomato.
  5. Next, place your Sliced Cucumber.
  6. Add Sliced Chicken Strips across the roll.
  7. Sprinkle Diced Carrot all over.
  8. Finally, lather with Chipotle Sauce.
  9. Congratulations, you are done!

Enjoy the most delicious lunch ever!
